My Interests

Outside of school and programming, I have a few interests that help me grow personally and professionally. These areas keep me disciplined, curious, and focused on long-term improvement.

Investing & Financial Literacy

How long I've been interested:
I started learning about personal finance and investing in the past few years and have been gradually building my knowledge since then.

What I've learned:
I've focused on understanding ETFs, index funds, compound interest, and long-term investing strategies. I've also learned more about budgeting, financial discipline, and how to evaluate risk. This interest has helped me think more long-term and make more informed decisions with money.

Future goals:
I want to continue learning about the stock market, expand into more detailed analysis, and eventually build a diversified portfolio that supports long-term financial stability.

Fitness

How long I've done it:
I have been consistently training for a few years through gym workouts, mobility work, and general conditioning.

What I learned:
Fitness taught me consistency, goal-setting, and discipline. It has also helped me understand the importance of balancing training intensity with recovery and nutrition.

Future goals:
I want to continue improving my overall conditioning, flexibility, and lifestyle habits. Fitness is something I want to maintain long-term.

Strength Training

How long I've done it:
I have been strength training for a few years, focusing on building muscle, form, and functional movement.

What I learned:
Strength training taught me patience, proper technique, and how to progressively overload safely. I've also learned a lot about biomechanics, form cues, and how small adjustments can improve performance.

Future goals:
I want to continue increasing my strength, improving my mobility, and maintaining a balanced training routine that supports both performance and overall health.
